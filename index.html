About

I am a data-driven problem solver with a strong passion for transforming complex data into impactful, real-world solutions. With over two years of professional experience as a Data Engineer, I have built and optimized scalable data pipelines, worked extensively with structured and unstructured data, and collaborated across teams to deliver reliable, production-ready data systems. This industry experience has given me a solid foundation in data engineering, analytics, and machine learning workflows.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Data Science, where I am deepening my expertise in advanced analytics, machine learning, statistical modeling, and data-driven decision-making. Alongside my academic journey, I work as a Graduate Researcher, actively contributing to applied NLP research in the healthcare domain, where I focus on extracting meaningful insights from clinical and health-related text data to support better decision-making and outcomes.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, and real-world impact, particularly in domains where data can directly improve human lives. I enjoy bridging the gap between theory and practice, designing end-to-end solutions that move from raw data to interpretable insights and deployable models.

Beyond technical skills, I bring a mindset rooted in continuous learning, resilience, and curiosity. I firmly believe that consistent learning and a never-giving-up attitude are what truly set individuals apart and drive them toward meaningful goals. Every challenge is an opportunity to grow, and every dataset tells a story waiting to be uncovered.

Through this portfolio, I showcase my projects, research work, and technical expertise that reflect my journey as a data professional, one who is committed to learning, innovation, and creating value through data.
